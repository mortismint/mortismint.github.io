You're welcome to reupload my music to free platforms like SoundCloud or YouTube. Please do not upload to commercial platforms such as Spotify or Apple Music — doing so violates the Creative Commons BY-NC-SA license my music is released under.

When reuploading, you must give appropriate credit — include the track title, author, and a source link to the original alongside the license type.

Failure to follow these guidelines may result in a copyright takedown.
